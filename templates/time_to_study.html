Now to plan how you're going to use that time.

Let me guess. You know all about smart goals. (Linc amirite). You just don't know exactly how to set them. Try this...


Step 1 - what to work on

Get a piece of paper, right now, and divide it into columns.

At the top of each column, write down your exam subjects.

Follow these links for each of your exams subjects. Then return here.


For science subjects (and math) For essay subjects For languages subjects

Step 2 - when to do it

You should have written down your weak areas and time needed for each subject

Now you need to allocate some time.

You said you were going to study {{study_time}} hours before your exam. Which day or days of the week will that be?

Are you going to have a specific day for certain subjects?



Step 3 - accountability

Now you know what, and you know when.

Grab you calender (physical or digital)

Write in your study time

Go tell your mum what you're going to study and when.

It's time to get your bum in that chair and start studying.
