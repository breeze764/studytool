Find your weak areas

  • Get a piece of paper and divide it into columns
  • At the top of each column, write an exam subject you take
  • For each subject*, go to the relevant link below
  • Write down the checklist
  • Rate each point on the checklist out of five, based on your confidence
  • If you aren't sure about the point, try explaining it out loud. Can't explain it? That's a one

  • *Ag, Hort, and Earth and Space Sciences, are also Stem Subjects.
