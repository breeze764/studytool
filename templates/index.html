So you made it to year 12...

You're probably wondering what exams are like.

And if you need to start studying yet.

And also... how to study...?

Don't panic. Follow the interactive tutorials on this website, and (hopefully) I guarantee you'll leave with a plan in hand.


Here are the steps

1. Find out what to study (ie. complex circuits, verb tenses, practice essays)

2. Decide when (ie. two half-hour sprints after dinner, an hour each morning)

3. Learn how (i.e. the SQ3R method, retrieval strategies, interleaving practices)



Go!
