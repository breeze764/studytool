Checklist for Essays

Text



English*

  • Key facts such as director/author, year of release ect
  • Confident discussing 1-2 aspects like theme and character
  • Memorized 4-6 quotes/evidence

History and Media

  • Key dates a names
  • Understand real world significance/connections of topic
  • Memorized 5-10 key pieces of evidence

For all Essays

  • 2-3 timed practice essays using memorized evidence
  • Teacher feedback on essays
  • Practice paragraphs for common questions
  • Read and annotated past exemplars
  • Confident with paragraph structure


*The English close reading standard is extra special. Please click here.
